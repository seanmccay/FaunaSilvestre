Get in touch with Fauna Silvestre Studio

There are multiple ways to get a hold of the studio. Feel free to use the provided points of contact and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

Etsy
Please check out our Etsy store and browse what we have available. You can send us messages here and we will be able to get in touch with you.
Email
Please send an email to shelby@faunasilvestrestudio.com

Also feel free to send emails with questions about the art. If you would like to make a request, it is helpful if you include information about what you would like.

Include things such as:

  • Reference photos

    This can be a photo of an animal in a certain pose, a landscape photo, or anything to help us understand what you would like.

  • Preferred mediums

    Would you like an acryllic painting? A drawing colored with pencils? Maybe a multi-media project?

  • Other information

    What dimensions will the project need to be? What sort of artistic direction are we trying to go in?
